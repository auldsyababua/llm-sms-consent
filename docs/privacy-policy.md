**Last updated:** April 24, 2026

This program is "Colin's LLM SMS Assistant" (personal, non-commercial). I collect phone numbers and message content solely to deliver conversational responses.

**Data collected**
- Phone number
- SMS message content and timestamps

**How it is used**
- Messages are processed to generate responses from the LLM assistant
- Messages are routed and delivered by Twilio

**Sharing and marketing**
- I do **not** sell or share your information with third parties
- I do **not** use this data for advertising or marketing

**Retention**
- Messages may be retained for a limited time to operate the assistant (for example, to maintain conversation context)

Questions? Text **HELP** or open an issue: https://github.com/auldsyababua/llm-sms-consent/issues
